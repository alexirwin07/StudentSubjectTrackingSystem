What is SSTS?

SSTS (Student Subject Traking System) is a project in which it's purpose is to aid students in a way that they can easily track progress in the subjects they study. The way this works is by tracking the grades students achieve in their studies (obtained through the user's input) and applying this information to present a visual representation of the progress they have made.
